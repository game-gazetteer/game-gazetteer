Accessibility Statement

Game Gazetteer is committed to providing an accessible experience for all users, including those with disabilities. We strive to ensure that our interactive maps and content are usable by as many people as possible.

Accessibility Goals

Our goal is to make Game Gazetteer accessible in accordance with generally recognized accessibility standards, including the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG), where reasonably possible.

Measures We Support

  • Readable fonts and clear color contrast
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation where possible
  • Semantic HTML structure for screen readers
  • Scalable text and responsive layouts
  • Descriptive alternative text for key images

Known Limitations

Some interactive map features rely on third-party libraries or custom graphics that may not yet be fully accessible to all assistive technologies. We acknowledge these limitations and aim to improve accessibility over time.

Third-Party Content

Game Gazetteer uses third-party services and libraries, including mapping and authentication tools, which may not fully conform to accessibility standards. We do not control the accessibility of third-party content.

Feedback & Contact

If you encounter accessibility barriers while using Game Gazetteer, we welcome your feedback. Please reach out through the official Game Gazetteer website so we can review and address the issue where feasible.

We view accessibility as an ongoing effort and appreciate your patience as the site continues to evolve.
